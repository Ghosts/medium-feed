Medium-Feed

A simple utility to get article objects for any Medium user.

Usage

Initialize a feed:

var mediumFeed = new MediumFeed(); //Setup without username
var mediumFeed = new MediumFeed("caden"); //Include username for default feed

Get articles:

mediumFeed.getFeed("caden"); //Pass username of the feed you want
mediumFeed.getFeed(); //Use username passed to MediumFeed constructor
